Services

Motor Repair & Rewind Capabilities

• A documented best practices rewind performed by a highly qualified service provider can lead to significant improvements in a motor's power density and reliability

• In many cases, having a motor rewound can help users lower operating costs, improve motor reliability and uptime and an improve return on investment.

Cage Winding Rebuilding Capabilities

• Redesign & Modification to squirrel cage winding to suit driven equipments to avoid repeated failures.

• Modification in slot design to suit higher copper cross sectional area by re-notching the existing slot/ laminations.

• Silver brazing of cage winding to short circuit rings under controlled temperatures.

Traction Machines Rewind Capabilities

• Refurbishing, rewind of armature, main field, Interpole coils with Class of Insulation F & H.

• Rebuilding of commutator and reinsulation.

• Fabricating new commutator in moulded and fabricated design.

• Fabricating new brush gear assemblies.

Rotor Cage Winding Design Capabilities

• Modification of rotor slot design of 550 HP, 3300 Volts, 6 Pole, Crompton make, I.R. Compressor Motor. Change in Rotor bar design was carried out to avoid repeated failure of squirrel cage winding

• We redesign the rotor cage winding to suit site operational condition

Rebuilding Stator Capabilities

• Rebuilding stator core in older machines by replacing dynamo grade steel with CRNO grade to improve iron losses.

• Rebuilding segmental core in new stator frame.

Machine Testing / Diagnostics

• AC & DC High Voltage.

• Measurement of Tan Delta.

• Measurement of IR & PI.

• Conducting Core Test.

• Conducting NDT test on components.
